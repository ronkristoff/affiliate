What is Affiliate Tracking?

Understanding how software knows who sent whom

The Problem We're Solving

Imagine you run a SaaS business. You want other people to sell your product for you — so you create an affiliate program.

Here's the challenge: when someone signs up through an affiliate's link, how does your software know which affiliate gets credit?

💡
Think of it like a referral at a restaurant

When your friend refers you to a restaurant and you mention their name, the restaurant knows to give your friend credit (maybe a free drink). Affiliate tracking does the same thing — but automatically, and at scale.

The affiliate tracking flow answers three questions:

1
Who sent this visitor?

Detecting which affiliate's link was clicked

2
Did they actually sign up?

Recording when a visitor becomes a customer

3
Who gets paid?

Creating commission records and payout batches
