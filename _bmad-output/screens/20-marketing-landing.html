The problem

Managing affiliate commissions manually
is costing you — and your affiliates.

  • 📋
    Plan upgrades go untracked
    Every time a referred customer upgrades their plan, that delta should trigger a commission. If you're not on SaligPay-native tooling, that never happens automatically.
  • 🔌
    Webhooks you have to maintain yourself
    Every competitor connects to your billing via a third-party webhook. That's connector lag, reliability risk, and an integration you own — forever.
  • 🔄
    Refunds don't reverse commissions
    A customer refunds. Did you remember to reverse the affiliate commission? Probably not. Now you've overpaid, and the ledger is wrong.
  • 📊
    Spreadsheets break the moment you scale
    10 affiliates is manageable in a spreadsheet. 50 is chaos. You need a real system before you hit the wall — not after.
